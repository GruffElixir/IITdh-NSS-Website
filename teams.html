About

Welcome to the official website of the National Service Scheme (NSS) at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Dharwad. Established with the aim of promoting social responsibility and community engagement among students, NSS IIT Dharwad is dedicated to making a positive impact on society through various outreach programs, initiatives, and collaborations. Our website serves as a platform to showcase our activities, share our vision and values, and engage with the community.
